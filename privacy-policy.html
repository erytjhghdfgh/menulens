2. Purpose of Collection and Use

  • User authentication via Google Sign-In and verification of service eligibility
  • Providing AI menu analysis (uploaded images are sent to the Google Gemini API)
  • Collecting and processing feedback for service improvement
  • Using menu image data for AI model training (after anonymization)
  • Preventing misuse and maintaining service stability
📌 Important: Uploaded menu images are sent to the Gemini API (Google) for analysis. Google's Privacy Policy also applies.

4. Use of Data for AI Training

MenuLens may use image data as follows to improve service quality.

  • Uploaded menu images may be used as AI model training data.
  • Data used for AI training has all personal identifiers removed (anonymized), including email and UID.
  • Upon account deletion, personal identifiers are immediately removed and only anonymized images are retained.
  • Please avoid uploading images containing personal information (e.g., receipts, ID cards).
📌 Consent Notice: By signing in and using the service, you are deemed to have agreed to this policy. Separate consent is obtained for the use of data in AI training.

6. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time.

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (account deletion)
  • Withdraw consent to AI training data use (contact us by email)

7. Account Deletion & Data Handling

Upon account deletion, your data is handled as follows.

  • Immediately deleted: Firebase account (email, name, profile photo, UID)
  • Retained in anonymized form: Uploaded menu images (after removal of personal identifiers)
  • Retained in anonymized form: Submitted feedback (author info removed; content only retained)

8. Security Measures

  • Access control via Firebase Security Rules
  • Secure authentication based on Google OAuth 2.0
  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS
  • Minimal collection principle — only data necessary for service operation is collected

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, users will be notified in advance via in-app notices or email. Changes take effect 7 days after the notice is published.
